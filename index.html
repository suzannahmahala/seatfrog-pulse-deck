Introducing

Pulse

A living intelligence system that automatically collects what passengers are saying, alongside industry trends, TOC-specific data, and competitor activity - classifies it all with AI, and lets anyone on the team ask questions and get evidence-backed answers.

The opportunity.

We already have seatfrog-reviews in Slack, which pulls in app reviews, Iterate survey data, and web forum mentions. It's a great start - but it's only part of the picture.

Zendesk tickets, user research, industry reports, and competitor activity all live in separate tools and teams. There's no single place to get the full picture of what's happening across our customers, the industry, and the competition.

Pulse solves this by bringing every source of customer, competitor, and industry insight into one AI-classified system - and making it queryable in plain English. Instead of trawling through tools, you ask a question and get the answer.

Three tools. One intelligence system.

Pulse collects everything, classifies it with AI, and surfaces it through three interfaces designed for different needs.

The Map

See the whole picture.

A live, interactive journey map that shows passenger pain points across 7 stages of the full rail experience - from considering travel through to arrival and loyalty.

Every pain point is sourced

Real quotes from real passengers, tagged by severity (critical, high, medium) and linked to the data source - reviews, surveys, CS tickets, or industry research.

Industry context built in

Industry-wide stats from ORR, DfT, and Transport Focus sit alongside customer feedback, so you can see where Seatfrog fits into the bigger picture.

Where we win, where we don't

Each pain point is tagged with Seatfrog's position: solves, partially addresses, gap, or opportunity. Clear view of what to prioritise.

Always current

Fed by live data - review bot sentiment, CS tickets, survey responses. Not a one-off report; a living document that updates as new data flows in.

V1 is live now - built on 4,741 survey responses, 6 weeks of review monitoring, CS data, and industry research.

The Sparring Partner

Ask a question. Get evidence.

A conversational AI loaded with all of our customer, industry, and competitor data - accessible through Claude, the AI tool we already use. Ask in plain English, get back answers backed by real quotes and numbers.

"What should we prioritise fixing at the pre-travel stage?"
